Certificate I

AI Fluency for Financial Services

Move every banking and advisory professional from ad-hoc AI use to structured, role-specific productivity. The foundational certificate for all 1,160 Long Island employees.

5 weeks · ~20 hours For: All roles Levels 2 → 4
MODULE 01

Structured Prompting for Client Work

Context engineering, role-based prompts, and iterative refinement. Build AI queries that produce compliant meeting prep, client briefings, and research summaries in your first session.

MODULE 02

AI Workspaces & Persistent Context

Create dedicated AI environments loaded with compliance guidelines, client history templates, and product documentation. Stop rebuilding context in every conversation.

MODULE 03

AI-Assisted Research & Synthesis

Use AI research tools to synthesize public filings, news, and market data into one-page prospect briefs. Turn 45 minutes of meeting prep into 10.

MODULE 04

Compliant AI in Regulated Finance

What AI can draft and what humans must decide. Suitability guardrails, disclosure boundaries, audit trail thinking, and judgment calls in advisory, lending, and KYC workflows.

MODULE 05

CRM, Follow-Up & Referral Workflows

AI-powered Salesforce hygiene, automated follow-up drafts, cross-referral identification, and digital onboarding coaching. Connect client conversations to systematic action.

After this certificate, your employee can prepare for any client meeting in 10 minutes instead of 45, produce compliant follow-up materials in seconds, maintain disciplined CRM records, and use AI with the professional judgment to know exactly what requires human review.

Certificate II

AI Leadership for Financial Services

Move high-potential managers and senior advisors from personal AI productivity to designing AI-enabled workflows for their teams. The leadership accelerator.

6 weeks · ~25 hours For: Managers, senior advisors, high-potentials Prerequisite: Certificate I Levels 4 → 5
MODULE 01

From Tools to Systems

The builder mindset: map an existing manual workflow (weekly branch report, client onboarding, prospect list), identify the bottleneck, and redesign it with AI at the center.

MODULE 02

Performance Dashboards & Client Analytics

Build branch performance visualizations using AI-assisted tools. Track acquisition, digital adoption, referral conversion, and portfolio growth — then act on what you see.

MODULE 03

Client Segmentation & Predictive Thinking

Segment your digitally active client base by value, risk, and opportunity. Understand lead scoring, churn signals, and next-best-action logic without writing a line of code.

MODULE 04

Supervised AI Workflows

Design human-in-the-loop AI processes using no-code platforms: automated onboarding sequences, compliance screening, report generation, and escalation routing — all with audit trails.

MODULE 05

Measuring ROI & Building the Business Case

Quantify AI impact: hours saved, error reduction, revenue influenced. Build and present a transformation proposal to market leadership that gets funded, not filed.

After this certificate, your leader can design, implement, and measure an AI-enabled workflow that reduces manual work by 30% or more in their team — and present the business case to market leadership in language that drives budget approval.
