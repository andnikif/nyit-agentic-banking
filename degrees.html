Custom MBA

MBA in AI-Driven Financial Leadership

Same accredited MBA curriculum. Custom application for Bank of America professionals. Every course teaches financial professionals to lead with data and AI — not just manage with intuition.

30–45 credits 24 months part-time 8 core + concentration Levels 3 → 5

What Your Manager Can Do After Each Course

CourseAfter This Course, Your Manager Can…
ACCT 601
Managerial Accounting		 Break down the P&L of any branch or product line, isolate what drives costs versus revenue, and present it to market leadership with AI-generated visualizations — not just raw spreadsheets.
ECON 601
Managerial Economics		 Model how a rate change, a new competitor, or a demographic shift will affect their local market — and recommend a strategic response backed by data, not instinct.
FINC 601
Financial Management		 Evaluate whether a branch investment (new hire, renovation, digital kiosk) is worth the capital — running AI-assisted scenario analysis and sensitivity testing on real numbers.
MGMT 605
Organizational Behavior		 Lead their team through AI adoption resistance. Diagnose why people resist, design incentives that work, and coach a multigenerational workforce through technology transitions.
MRKT 620
Strategic Marketing		 Design a client acquisition strategy using AI-driven segmentation — not “call more people” but “call these people, for these reasons, with this message.”
QANT 630
Operations & SCM		 Map their branch’s client service process, find the bottleneck, and redesign it — reducing wait times, improving NPS, and freeing advisor time for high-value conversations.
BUSA 630
Predictive Analytics		 Build a model that predicts which clients will attrite, which prospects will convert, and which products each client segment is most likely to need next — then act on it.
MGMT 650
Strategic Capstone		 Present a complete AI transformation proposal for their own team or market to BofA leadership — not a hypothetical case study, but a real plan they can implement the following quarter.

Custom MS

MS in FinTech & Data Analytics

For the people who will build your next generation of internal capabilities. AI models, compliance systems, client intelligence platforms — built in-house, not purchased from vendors.

30 credits · 10 courses 20–24 months part-time STEM designated Levels 4 → 6

What Your Builder Can Do After Each Course

CourseAfter This Course, Your Analyst / Builder Can…
BUSA 601
Data Storytelling		 Build interactive Tableau or Power BI dashboards that any branch manager can use to see client trends, performance alerts, and portfolio metrics — replacing emailed spreadsheets with live intelligence.
BUSA 602
Programming		 Write Python and SQL scripts that automate client data extraction, clean messy datasets, and generate formatted reports — tasks that currently take a team member a full day each week.
QANT 605
Statistical Thinking		 Design a rigorous A/B test for a branch initiative (e.g., “does this onboarding script improve 90-day retention?”) and tell you whether the result is real or statistical noise.
BUSA 660
AI & Machine Learning		 Build and validate a machine learning model that predicts client churn, flags high-risk accounts, or scores leads — not just use someone else’s model, but build one from your own data.
FINC 601
Financial Management		 Apply AI-enhanced financial analysis to evaluate lending decisions, capital projects, and portfolio construction — the financial judgment layer on top of the technical skills.
FINC 671
Blockchain & Digital Assets		 Evaluate whether a DeFi protocol, tokenized asset, or smart contract has a genuine use case for BofA clients — or whether it’s hype. Risk-first assessment, not speculation.
FINC 672
Algorithmic Trading		 Design, backtest, and evaluate quantitative investment strategies using real market data — directly applicable to Merrill’s advisory and research functions.
FINC 771
AI in Financial Services		 Build NLP models that analyze earnings calls, SEC filings, and research reports for investment signals. Build credit scoring models. Build AI-driven risk assessment tools. This is where “I use AI” becomes “I build AI for finance.”
FINC 772
RegTech & Cybersecurity		 Design a compliance monitoring workflow — automated flagging of suspicious patterns, regulatory reporting, audit trail generation — that operates within BofA’s governance framework.
FINC 773
FinTech Capstone		 Build a working FinTech prototype that solves a real Bank of America problem — client intelligence tool, automated compliance monitor, advisor preparation engine. BofA can serve as the industry sponsor.

After this MS, your innovation and analytics talent can build the AI-driven financial tools your bank needs — credit models, compliance monitors, client intelligence platforms, automated workflows — instead of waiting for vendors to sell you yesterday’s solution at tomorrow’s price.
